What Stroud District Council Gains

Why tools like this matter for GrowthHub support and reporting.
  • Clear view of pipeline strength – leads, conversions, and capacity are all visible, not hand-waved.
  • Evidence for funding decisions – the council can see whether assumptions are realistic before committing support.
  • Simple impact reporting – uplift in revenue, recurring income and jobs can be tracked per business or cohort.
  • Replicable framework – once calibrated for one founder, the same sheet can be used with other local businesses.
Revenue floor model (capacity-truthed): • 6 leads / month, q = 0.35, s = 0.35 • £2.5k sprints, £6k pilots, £1.5k retainers → ≈£3.7k / month in revenue and ≈£1.3k / month in recurring retainers after 12 months.

In the room: “These are realistic, not salesman-on-coke numbers. If anything, they’re a floor – if we execute well or demand is higher, the upside belongs to Stroud too.”
