Electric vehicle charging infrastructure statistics

Electric vehicle charging infrastructure statistics, including publicly available devices and grants provided by the Office for Zero Emission Vehicles (OZEV).

From:
Department for Transport and Office for Zero Emission Vehicles
Published
11 May 2021
Last updated
10 December 2025 — See all updates

Contents

  1. Latest monthly faster indicators for electric vehicle public charging devices
  2. Latest quarterly electric vehicle public charging infrastructure statistics
  3. Latest quarterly electric vehicle charging device grant scheme statistics
  4. About electric vehicle charging infrastructure statistics

Latest monthly faster indicators for electric vehicle public charging devices

Monthly faster indicators on the total number of electric vehicle public charging devices in the UK.

Monthly total number of electric vehicle public charging devices available in the UK, 1 December 2025

Data are sourced from the electric vehicle and charging point platform Zapmap.

Further information and accompanying methodology notes are published on the department’s Developing faster indicators of transport activity page.

Latest quarterly electric vehicle public charging infrastructure statistics

Quarterly statistics on the number of electric vehicle public charging devices in the UK, summarised by local authority and charging speed.

Electric vehicle public charging infrastructure statistics: October 2025

Data are sourced from the electric vehicle and charging point platform Zapmap.

This page has previously featured an interactive map displaying public charging infrastructure data at local authority level. This map has been withdrawn to allow resource for improving this and other products while a critical digital and data infrastructure transition takes place in the Department for Transport. All local authority level data provided by the map is available in tables 1a to 2b of the latest publication.

The data is also available in standard geographical formats from the department’s Transport Statistics Geography Portal. Users of the portal should note that whilst updated regularly, the dataset available may not represent the latest published charging device data.

Latest quarterly electric vehicle charging device grant scheme statistics

Quarterly statistics on the number of grants awarded for the installation of electric vehicle charging devices in the UK, funded by the Office for Zero Emission Vehicles (OZEV).

Electric vehicle charging device grant scheme statistics: October 2025

Data are sourced from administrative data and is used for operational purposes, with their statistical use secondary.

About electric vehicle charging infrastructure statistics

All statistics in the electric vehicle charging infrastructure statistics collection are official statistics in development.

See notes and definitions for further information on data sources.
