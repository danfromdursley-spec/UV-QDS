Quantum Dark Substrate (QDS) Supporting Observational Data
Daniel Brian Williams, Independent Researcher, United Kingdom, October 2025

This archive contains the empirical datasets and supporting figures used in the
Quantum Dark Substrate (QDS) analysis. Each file is described below.

CONTENTS:
-----------
1. rotmod.dat
   - SPARC galaxy rotation-curve residuals.
   - Source: Lelli et al. (2019), "One Law to Rule Them All", MNRAS 482, 5459.

2. sfb_LTG.zip
   - Surface-brightness and baryonic-mass profiles for late-type galaxies.
   - Source: SPARC database, McGaugh et al. (2016).

3. Figure_G5_QDS_vs_LambdaCDM_Performance.png
   - Bar chart showing performance comparison between QDS and Î›CDM across five
     metrics (Empirical Fit, Predictive Novelty, Parameter Efficiency, Rotation-
     Curve Accuracy, Credibility Index).

4. QDS_FigureG5_Data.csv
   - Numeric data underlying Figure G.5.
